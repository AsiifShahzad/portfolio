FreshTrack

An intelligent food management application that helps you track expiry dates and reduce food waste with AI-powered recipe suggestions.

Problem Statement

Food waste is a significant global issue, with approximately one-third of all food produced being wasted. A major contributor to household food waste is expired products that are forgotten in pantries and refrigerators. Additionally, consumers often struggle to utilize products before they expire, lacking inspiration for recipes that could incorporate these items.

Objectives

Reduce Food Waste

Help users track expiry dates of food items to ensure they're consumed before they expire, reducing household food waste.

Timely Notifications

Provide proactive alerts when products are approaching their expiry date, giving users time to plan meals accordingly.

Recipe Inspiration

Offer AI-powered recipe suggestions based on tracked items to help users creatively use products before they expire.

Key Features

Image Recognition

Add products by simply taking a photo. Our AI recognizes the product and automatically extracts expiry information.

Manual Entry

Quickly add items manually with an intuitive interface for entering product details and expiry dates.

Smart Alerts

Receive customizable notifications when products are approaching their expiry date, with adjustable timing preferences.

Chef AI

Get personalized recipe suggestions based on your tracked items, dietary preferences, and cooking skill level.

Waste Analytics

Track your progress in reducing food waste with insightful statistics and visualizations.

Cross-Device Sync

Access your inventory from any device with seamless cloud synchronization.
